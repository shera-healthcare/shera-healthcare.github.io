About us

Shera Healthcare is led by an experienced team of medical and surgical specialists who work following established internal processes and procedures as determined by individual departments to help attain their target wait times.
Our team, of clinicians and healthcare professionals, who have a vast amount of experience in all aspects of healthcare delivery within both the NHS and private healthcare sectors, include consultant surgeons, nursing staff and support technicians.
Vision
Our vision is to support our clients with their immediate challenges in order to enable them to achieve their long term-aspirations.
Mission
Our mission is to help Trusts and Healthcare boards meet the demands to run their services safely and efficiently with the patient as the centre of care.
