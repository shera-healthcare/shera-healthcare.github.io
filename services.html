Our Services

Shera works with NHS Trusts in several specialist areas. Upon determining pressures within a department, we work with the department to implement a sustainable plan in order to deliver a safe service.
Our specialist services include:
  • Ophthalmology – outpatient clinics, laser, injections, surgery including cataract & oculoplastics
  • Dermatology – outpatient clinics and minor procedures
  • Orthopeadics – outpatient clinics
  • Endoscopy services
