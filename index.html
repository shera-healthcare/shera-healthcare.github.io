Shera Healthcare is an insourcing company that was established to reduce wait-time pressures within the NHS. Our aim is to help departments improve clinical productivity professionally across a wide range of services while ensuring high-quality patient care is maintained.
Our teams work on weekends, when departments are not using clinical spaces or operating theatres, so we can treat patients without interfering with daily departmental activity.
